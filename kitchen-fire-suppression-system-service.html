About Kitchen Fire Suppression Systems

Exxell Fire Systems specializes in restaurant fire suppression systems. We have installed hundreds of systems, and tested and maintained thousands of systems.

Your kitchen system is designed to specifically handle fires created by oils in a kitchen. It can be activated by a pull station, or the automatic system in the hood that goes of when a certain temperature is reached.

Biannual testing and repairs is vital with your Kitchen Fire Suppression system to ensure if it is needed it will work effectively.
