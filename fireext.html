About Fire Extinguishers

Fire Extinguishers provide immediate response to a fire when it first starts. Fires have many different causes. The obvious ones are if you work with flammable chemicals, but they can ignite from many other causes such as faulty electrical.

Having a fire extinguisher to hand is a very good start. But it is very important to know if your fire extinguisher is working properly. This is why you preform monthly inspections mainly to see if it has been tampered with, and that the pressure is still in the green on the pressure gauge.

Annually you have your fire extinguisher checked by a licensed professional as there are many other things that can go wrong with a fire extinguisher over the course of a year such as the chemical inside becoming solidified, or the gauge breaking etc. Our fire extinguisher technicians have decades of experience and know all the codes concerning fire extinguishers.
