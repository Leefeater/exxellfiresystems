Fire Sprinkler System Inspection and Services

Fire sprinkler systems are designed to help prevent the spread of fire throughout a building. The sprinkler head is activated by a fusible link when heated to a certain temperature it sets off the system.

Your fire sprinkler system is connected to your alarm system, so if there is a fire the Fire Department is immediately notified and can follow up to contain the fire.

The testing and maintenance of your fire sprinkler system is of vital importance. Imagine if your sprinkler system went off but the alarm wasn't connected. The fire department has no way of knowing there is a fire in your building. Exxell Fire Systems knows fire sprinkler systems.

Annual Testing

During an annual test there are physical checks are performed on all the parts of your fire sprinkler system. These checks include water flow tests, control valve test, main drain test, visual check of all sprinklers and pipe.

5 Year Testing

During a five-year fire sprinkler inspection, physical checks are performed on all the parts of your fire sprinkler system. This includes all of the testing of the annual inspection but also includes a test of the check valves on your fire sprinkler system and a full flush of your Fire Department Connection (FDC).
