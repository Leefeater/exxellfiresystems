About Emergency Lights & Exit Signs

In events such as earthquakes or fires it is important to provide a path of egress from the building. This is why exit signs and emergency lights are of vital importance. They also provide needed light for response teams entering your building once you have left.

Emergency lights and exit signs are required to be able to run for up to 90 minutes with out any connection from the building's power. They are run by a back-up battery that has enough power to do this. But like all batteries when left unused they tend to die.

This is why you must have minimally have your emergency lights tested once a year to see if they do indeed run for 90 minutes, and also these tests exercises the battery and thus extend the life of the battery. It is also highly encouraged to do a 30 second test on them once a month to ensure they are working.
