About Fire Cache Rooms

Fire Cache Rooms, also known as Fire Department Supply Rooms, are required for buildings of 5 stories or more.

The items required for a Fire Cache Room are:

  • Fire Hose
  • EMS and First Aid Supplies
  • Fire Axes and Hand Tools
  • Smoke Ejectors
  • Breathing Equipment
  • Fire Cabinets
