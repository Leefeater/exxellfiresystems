About Kitchen Fire Suppression Systems

A kitchen is a hot place, with tons of activity and an open fire constantly. Your Fire Suppression System is designed to attack fires specifically created by cooking oils.

Every kitchen system is equip with a manual pull station and a Class K Fire Extinguisher so if a fire is getting out of hand you can use either as needed. It is also set up to go off automatically if a fire were to occur when nobody is around.

Biannual testing and repairs is vital with your Kitchen Fire Suppression system. With grease constantly being trapped in the nozzles and fusible links, if your system isn't kept up it may not work when you need it to.
